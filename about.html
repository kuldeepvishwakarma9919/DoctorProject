About Dr. Riya Sharma

Dr. Priya Sharma is a board-certified gynecologist with over 20 years of experience in women's healthcare. She completed her medical education at AIIMS Delhi and specialized training at Manipal Hospital.

Her practice focuses on providing compassionate, personalized care for women of all ages. Dr. Sharma is known for her gentle approach and dedication to patient education, ensuring that each woman understands her health options.

She is a Fellow of the Indian College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists and stays current with the latest advancements in women's health to provide the highest standard of care.
